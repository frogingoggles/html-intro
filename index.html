Evidence does not suggest abnormal urine analyses are significant

  • Treating Asymptomatic UA Increases Hospital Length Of Stay With No Benefit
  • Not treating bacteruria in patients with altered mental status rarely leads to worsening infection

Contrast Nephropathy from ct scan contrast is rare

  • The American College of Radiology and the National Kidney Foundation say contrast is low risk for gfr > 30 and likely overstate altogether
  • No contrast nephropathy occurred from cta pe in cohort

There is significant harm in using antipsychotics in delirious elderly patients

  • Antipsychotic use compared with non-use in adults with dementia was associated with increased risks of stroke, venous thromboembolism, myocardial infarction, heart failure, fracture, pneumonia, and acute kidney injury
